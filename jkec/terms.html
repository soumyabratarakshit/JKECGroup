Safety Measures


Prevention of accidents is a major aim of construction management both for human and financial considerations. Whatever the nature of construction projects may be. the accidents are likely to occur causing physical injury. Casualties and loss of money. In order to prevent accidents at construction sites. Safety measures need to be taken in the
    following major activities prone to risk of accidents.

    • Excavation
    • Demolition
    • Scaffolding, Ladders, Formwork
    • Use of equipment
    • Fabrication and erection
    • Storage
    • Hot bituminous works

      Plants to Achieve Safety Objective


    • Setting up of safety organization.
    • Need based safety training at all levels.
    • Conduct safety audit from time to time and implement recommendations of audit team.
    • Regularly organize site safety meetings. Make action plants on the recommendation of the committee and implement them.
    • Investigate each accident in detail and recommend preventive measures to the concerned project manager. Discuss the accidents in site safety committee meeting and implement recommendations to prevent accidents.
    • Remove hazards from time to time. Make use of necessary safety protective equipment and follow safe work practices.
    • Regular safety inspection trough “CHECKLISTS”.
    • Regular evaluation of Housekeeping of zones in sites.
    • Discuss each accident and draw the picture in the mind of the employees through letters or monthly meetings.
    • Plan and implement safety promotional activities like safety slogan competition. Safety posters competition and safety quiz.
    • Display safety posters in the sites.
